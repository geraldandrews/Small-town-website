Community Overview

Surrounded by beautiful mountains of the Appalachians, Rocky Gap is a community minded, family oriented, friendly, and safe small town for it's residents and visitors. In this small town, you can enjoy events, such as the annual Veteran's Day, Thanksgiving, and Christmas parade in November sponsored by the RG Ruritans, annual hotdog, barbeque, and bake sales, and easter egg hunts sponsored by some of it's residents, annual car shows, and other nearby attractions. Rocky Gap is also a great place to visit or live if you love fishing and the outdoors.
